Jen & Andy

It’s a simple story. We are a just a couple of kids that belong together. We met for the first time when we were about 18 at a concert (we both forget who was playing) through our mutual friend Erin. Although it was only to be a brief meeting, Jen was smitten and went home that night and wrote about Andy. Fast-forward about 5 years and we reconnected through our mutual friends Erin and Sam. This time, we both couldn’t stay away. After a couple years doing our own school adventures in Vancouver and Kingston, Andy joined Jen in Vancouver where we lived life to the fullest,

Ceremony & Reception

The Ceremony will take place at 5:00pm in Wascana Park behind the MacKenzie Art Gallery. There is ample parking at the gallery and on side streets around the area. In the event of major rain, we will move the ceremony indoors so look for signs if you aren’t sure.

If you come early to the ceremony, you will have a chance to do some treasure hunting! Check back for coordinates to put in a GPS App on your phone and search the area around the ceremony for a special token from us!

The reception will follow at 5:30pm in the Agra Torchinsky Salon at the MacKenzie Art Gallery. From 5:30 to 6:30, you can view the upstairs art gallery for free! Cocktails and appetizers will be provided in the Salon until the wedding party arrives around 6:30! Wear your party clothes, bring your dancing shoes and be ready to have a great time!.
